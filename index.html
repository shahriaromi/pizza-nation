Types of Pizza we pvoide

It’s easy to take pizza for granted. It’s your go-to when you need an easy meal at home or for an office event. A good pie can be elevated to gourmet cooking with the right combination of crust, sauce, cheese, toppings. We have the pizza statistics to prove it.

Cheese Pizza

It should be no shocker that a classic is the statistical favorite. Cheese pizza is one of the most popular choices by customers. Filled with gravy spicy sauce. It will always be a simple, unadorned masterpiece on its own.

Veggie Pizza

When you want to jazz up your cheese pizza with color and texture, veggies are the perfect topping. Everything from peppers and mushrooms, to eggplant and onions make for an exciting and tasty veggie pizza.

BBQ Chicken Pizza

If you love BBQ chicken and you love pizza, why not put them together? This has long been a cult favorite of sports fans and college kids. Chicken slathered over the top of a pie gives it a tangy, sweet flavor that can’t be beaten.
